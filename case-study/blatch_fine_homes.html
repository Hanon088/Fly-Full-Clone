The Challenge

Blatch Fine Homes; a premium property estate agency specialising in high calibre homes approached Fly Full circle to voice their concerns with the old look & feel of their website. We worked with them to rework their existing website to include more of a fluid user journey & a smarter layout to offer more of a professional image.

Launch Project

Minimal & modern approach

We sat down with Blatch Fine Homes and following an open & honest chat, we could hone in on the exact elements they wanted to improve and streamline. Having to showcase luxury houses, we wanted the website be an extension of their unique approach and showcase their high-quality service.

We designed a site that allows customers to browse a range of properties without compromising on a personal aesthetic and culture.
